Monday Night Pinball

Monday Night Pinball is a friendly competitive pinball bar league that aims to build interest in the hobby and encourage players and their teams to grow together.

Spring 2019 Updates

  • Team and Division Changes:
    • Drain Freeze at The Ice Box: retaining the name of the disbanded Narwhal Drain Freeze, several players from that team branded together to form a new team with a new home location.
    • Tilt & Destroy at Coindexter's: Coinhole Surfers disbanded, and this new team fills the void to keep Coindexter's as a two-team location.
    • Slap Kraken Pop is a new team at a new location, the Kraken Bar and Lounge in the U district. While the location has been around for years, having a full gameroom is a welcomed new addition!
    • Eighteen Ball Deluxe and Seacorns promoted to Division 1.
    • Stranger Slings changes their name to match their initials DTP, and are relegated back to Division 2 that they won two seasons ago.
    • The B Team at 4B's disbanded.
    • Division 1 remains at 12 teams and Division 2 remains at 14 teams.
  • Rule Changes:
